About the Dodo (Raphus cucullatus)

The dodo (Raphus cucullatus) is an extinct flightless bird that was endemic to the island of Mauritius, which is east of Madagascar in the Indian Ocean.

DNA & Methods of use to bring extinct spicies back to life

With animals DNA is generally inserted into using microinjection, it can be injected through the cell's nuclear envelope directly into the nucleus, or through the use of viral vectors.
